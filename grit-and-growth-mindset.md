## Q1) Grit
Grit means IQ, talent, and quick learning are not enough to succeed. To push through failures, I must develop a growth mindset and stay consistent with passion and perseverance.

## Q2) Growth Mindset
A growth mindset means I believe I can build my skills and I am in control of improving my abilities. I focus on getting better instead of only focusing on outcomes, and I embrace effort and perseverance.

## Q3) Internal Locus of Control
Internal locus of control means I believe I am in control of my results through my actions and effort, not external factors. The key point is that when I take responsibility for outcomes, I stay motivated and improve.

## Q4) Key points to build Growth Mindset
- I believe I can figure it out  
- Question my assumptions (both negative and positive)  
- Honour the struggle  

## Q5) My ideas to take action and build Growth Mindset
- I believe that I can build skills and improve.  
- I will focus on getting better instead of only focusing on outcomes.  
- I will put in effort consistently and embrace perseverance.  
- I will not get discouraged by failures and I will not take them personally.  
- I appreciate feedback and use it to improve.  
- I will maintain an internal locus of control mindset: **I am in control**.
