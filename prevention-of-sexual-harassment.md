# Sexual Harassment Prevention

## Question 1. What kinds of behaviour cause sexual harassment?

Physical: Aggressive acts, unwanted touching, forced closeness, obstruction
Verbal: Sexually suggestive comments, intrusive questions, demands, teasing, insulting terms
Visual: Online harassment, sexual gestures, explicit content, hostile staring
Quid Pro Quo: Threats of harm, promises of favors

## Question 2. What would you do in case you face or witness any incident or repeated incidents of such behaviour?
### If Harassed Personally:
* Speak up immediately
* Record all details & save digital proof
* File a formal complaint with Internal Committee
### If Witnessing Harassment:
* Use Bystander Intervention: Delegate, Document, Delay, Direct
* Support the victim and report the incident
### If Behaviour Repeats:
* Report to Internal Committee
* Escalate if no action is taken
* Use SHe-Box as last resort
