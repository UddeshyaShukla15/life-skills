# Focus Management

## Question 1. What is Deep Work?
Deep work is a concept given by Cal Newport it means Working with full focus on one important task without distractions.

## Question 2. According to author how to do deep work properly, in a few points?
* Set a fixed time period for focused work
* Do only one task at a time
* Decide your goal before starting work

## Question 3. How can you implement the principles in your day to day life?
* By keeping phone away while studying or working
* Taking short breaks after focus sessions
* Not using social media during work time

## Question 4. What are the dangers of social media, in brief?
* It reduces focus and concentration
* Wastes time unknowingly
* Can become addictive
* It disturbs sleep at night
