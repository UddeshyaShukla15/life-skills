# Good practices for Software Development

## Question 1. Which point(s) were new to you?
One new thing for me was how important it is to ask questions in the right way. I understood that just saying “it’s not working” is not helpful. We should clearly explain the issue and mention what we already tried

## Question 2. Which area do you think you need to improve on? What are your ideas to make progress in that area?
* Sometimes I get distracted easily. I need to work with full concentration.
* I should explain my problem clearly and tell what I already tried before asking for help.
* I need to plan my tasks better so I don’t rush at the end.
* I should participate more actively and not just listen quietly.
